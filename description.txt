Obama Camera Overhaul is a mod that allows you to chance how camera works

Feature :
- Screen shake on hurt
- Screen shake on kill enemy
- Screen shake on hit enemy
- Camera follow on boss and player
- Camera lock on boss
- Death cam
- Smooth Camera
- Quick Look at Boss Hotkey

all of the feature here is configurable :)
