Obama Camera is a mod that allows you to chance how camera works

Feature :
- Screen shake on hurt
- Screen shake on kill
- Camera follow on boss and player
- Camera lock on boss
- Death cam
- Smooth Camera

all of the feature here is configurable :)

Note : its problaby not the best idea to use this mod and overhaul cuz it may conflict each other :/
